      Healthy Carrot Cake Baked Oatmeal

      A delightful blend of carrot cake flavors and hearty baked oats, this dish is perfect for breakfast or a satisfying snack.
      Pr√©paration 15 min
      Cuisson 45 min

      Portions 6 servings

      Ingr√©dients

      • 200 g rolled oats (gluten-free if required)
      • 1 teaspoon baking powder
      • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
      • 0.5 teaspoon ground ginger
      • 0.25 teaspoon fine sea salt
      • 420 ml almond milk or other non-dairy milk
      • 2 large eggs
      • 80 ml pure maple syrup
      • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
      • 75 g freshly shredded carrots
      • 80 g raisins, divided
      • 60 g chopped pecans or other nuts, divided

      Instructions

      1. Preheat your oven to 375F and grease an 8x8 inch casserole dish.
      2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger, and salt.
      3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, eggs, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth.
      4. Stir in the shredded carrots along with half of the raisins and pecans.
      5. Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients and fold gently until just combined.
      6. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and flatten the surface.
      7. Sprinkle the remaining raisins and pecans on top and lightly press them into the mixture.
      8. Bake uncovered for 40 to 45 minutes until golden brown and set.
      9. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving warm or store in the fridge for later.
